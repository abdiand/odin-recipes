Biggie's Southern Mac and Cheese

What is Southern Macaroni and Cheese?

Well, its basically a custard-style baked mac and cheese with eggs and evaporated milk.This version uses three kinds of cheese and butter and a little sour cream.It bakes up creamy but firm, it is hearty and delicious.

Ingredients List

  • milk
  • sour cream
  • butter
  • three cheese types
  • evaporated milk

Recipe Instructions

  1. Drain and Cook elbow macaroni, place it in a pan or baking dish
  2. Make the custard mixture by whisking together evaporated milk,milk, eggs and some seasoning
  3. Pour mixture over macaroni
  4. Heat oven to 350Â°F
  5. Top with more groated cheese and bake until the top is browned
  6. Remove from oven and let it cool down until the macaroni and cheese is fully set. (10-15min)
